How It Works

  1. Step 1: Register Online
    Participants must fill out the registration form to officially join the competition. You can apply solo or as a team of up to 3 members.
  2. Step 2: Submit Your Business Idea
    Once registered, teams will submit a short written proposal outlining their startup idea, its impact, and the target audience.
  3. Step 3: Semi-Finalist Selection
    Judges will review all submissions and shortlist the most promising ideas. Selected semi-finalists will be notified by email.
  4. Step 4: Mentorship + Pitch Prep
    Semi-finalist teams will attend a 1-hour virtual mentorship session with experienced student entrepreneurs to refine their pitch.
  5. Step 5: Live Semi-Final Pitch
    Semi-finalists will present a 5-minute pitch live on Zoom. Judges will score based on innovation, clarity, and feasibility.
  6. Step 6: Finalist Announcement
    Top teams from the semi-final will move on to the final round. They will be given one more week to enhance their pitch.
  7. Step 7: Final Pitch + Winner Selection
    Finalists will compete in a live finale. Judges will evaluate pitches, provide feedback, and announce the winners.
